Story 1: Specify the Business Problem

In today's digital world, users seek:

Motivational quotes

Love quotes

Success quotes

Sad quotes

Random inspiration

However, users waste time searching manually.

 Problem Statement

Develop an NLP-based chatbot that:

Understands user mood or request

Classifies intent

Recommends relevant quotes instantly

 Story 2: Business Requirements
Functional Requirements:

Detect user intent (motivation, love, success, etc.)

Recommend appropriate quotes

Handle greetings and fallback queries

Support interactive conversation

Non-Functional Requirements:

Fast response time

Scalable

Easy to update with new quotes

High intent classification accuracy

Story 3: Literature Survey
Technologies Studied:

Rule-based chatbots

NLP-based chatbots

Machine learning intent classification

Deep learning conversational models

 Framework Studied:

Rasa – For intent classification & dialogue management

Dialogflow – Google’s chatbot platform

NLTK – Python NLP toolkit

spaCy – Advanced NLP processing

 Conclusion from Literature Review:

Rule-based systems lack flexibility.

NLP-based intent recognition provides better accuracy.

Rasa allows full customization and offline development.
